Welcome to Ivory32

Maxillofacial imaging and Diagnostics.

Our Aim

We are the first -state of the art dedicated maxillofacial diagnostic centre for 2d and 3d imaging of the maxillofacial region in Western UP.

We aim to provide advanced diagnocis information in UP to better aid in the patient care and management.

Best Services of Our Hospital

Department of General Medicine

The medicine department caters to patient's routine illnesses and specifically to cardiac conditions. With a day care facility, patients can be admitted for day care intravenous medications. The department has a ECG and a TMT unit for cardiac evaluation.

Department of pathology and micro biology

The department caters to all kinds of routine blood and urine sampling and investigations. Our separate window for maxillofacial patients and attractive pre surgical packages are a hit amongst practioners. The department also has a histopathologic unit dealing with all kinds of biopsies.

Department of Maxillofacial Radiology and Medicine

This department focuses on cbct scans and digital opg scans. The cbct scans are a gold standard investigation of choice for bony pathologies and maxillofacial trauma. Also the cbct is a must requirement before dental implant placement. The department caters to implant planning of all companies and takes pride in quick and efficient online reports to the doctors as well as patients. The oral medicine department deals with management or oral cancers, precancers, oral ulcerative conditions, tobacco cessation and TMJ disorders.
